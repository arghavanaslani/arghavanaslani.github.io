This is a space where I try to make sense of what I do — and share it with whoever finds it useful. I'm a master's student in Cognitive Science in Paris, originally from Iran, with a background in Electrical Engineering. My work lives at the intersection of brains and algorithms.
I started building this site with PhD applications in mind. But somewhere along the way it became something else. Expect plain explanations of complex ideas, honest takes on life in science, and the occasional post about things that go beyond the lab.
